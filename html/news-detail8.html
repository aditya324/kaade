Every Mom Deserves a Birth Plan – Make Yours with Us Because your delivery day should feel like your day, not a surprise.

Introduction

“Will it be painful?”

“Can I choose a normal delivery?”

“Will my husband be allowed in the room?”

Pregnancy comes with dreams — but delivery often arrives with fear. A birth plan is not a luxury — it’s your right. And at Kaade Hospital, we help you build one that fits your comfort, choices, and safety.

What is a Birth Plan?

It’s a simple guide that covers:

• Your preferred delivery method (Normal / C-section)

• Pain management options (natural, epidural, etc.)

• Breastfeeding struggles

• Who you want in the room with you

• Body image concerns

• Baby care preferences post delivery (skin-to-skin, breastfeeding support)

Why It Matters:

• Reduces fear and last-minute confusion

• Empowers mothers with control and clarity

• Helps doctors and staff give you the best experience

At Kaade Hospital, You Can Expect:

• 2-bed advanced labor suite with privacy

• Female gynecologists & experienced OBG team

• Emergency-ready operation theatres

• Birth partner access & emotional support

• Friendly nurses who respect your wishes

Whether it's your first or third baby — you deserve to be heard.

Final Word

It’s your birth. Your baby. Your choice. Let’s plan it your way, with expert hands beside you.

Book your birth planning session today at Kaade Hospital. DM or call us — Your motherhood journey, your way.
