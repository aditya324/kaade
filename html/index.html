Super Specilaties

Kaade Hospital offers a variety of specialized services to address specific health needs:

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department provides comprehensive, compassionate care using advanced diagnostics and personalized treatment plans.

Diabetology

Our Diabetology department offers comprehensive, personalized care for diabetes management using advanced diagnostics and tailored treatment strategies.

General Surgery

Our General Surgery department uses advanced, minimally invasive techniques for safe, effective care and rapid recovery.

Dermatology

Our Dermatology department uses advanced diagnostic and treatment methods for effective skin care. Our dedicated team provides personalized care to promote optimal skin health.

24X7 pediatrics and NICU

Our Pulmonology department provides advanced diagnostic and therapeutic care for respiratory conditions.

Obstetrics and Gynaecology

Our Obstetrics and Gynaecology department delivers comprehensive, personalized care for women's health, ensuring safe pregnancies and effective management of reproductive concerns.

Spinecare Clinic

Specializing in comprehensive spinal health, our Spinecare department combines advanced diagnostics with tailored treatment plans, from non-surgical therapies to rehabilitation programs.

Orthopaedic and Trauma

Our Orthopaedic and Trauma department delivers comprehensive care for musculoskeletal injuries using advanced techniques.

Laparoscopy Surgery

Our Minimally Invasive Surgery department specializes in advanced laparoscopic procedures, offering faster recovery times, smaller incisions, and reduced discomfort compared to traditional open surgery.
