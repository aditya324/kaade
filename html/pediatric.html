Pediatric Clinic

Our Pediatric Clinic provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents up to 18 years of age. We offer comprehensive medical care focusing on growth, development, and the unique health needs of young patients in a child-friendly environment. Our team of experienced pediatricians is dedicated to ensuring your child's optimal health and well-being.

Our pediatric services include routine well-child checkups, immunizations, developmental screenings, and treatment of acute and chronic childhood illnesses. We manage conditions such as asthma, allergies, childhood infections, nutritional deficiencies, and behavioral concerns with specialized pediatric expertise.

Our clinic provides age-appropriate care from newborn evaluations through adolescent medicine, including school physicals, sports medicine, and mental health support. We emphasize preventive care and early intervention to promote healthy growth and development at every stage.

With child-friendly facilities and a compassionate approach, we create a comfortable environment for young patients. Our services include lactation counseling for new mothers, parenting guidance, and coordination with pediatric specialists when needed, ensuring comprehensive care for your child's health needs.

Kaade Hospital today stands as a testimony to a commitment of competent and affordable medical care thus giving the best outcome, for everyone, everytime.
