Welcome to Kaade Hospital

Kaade Hospital happens to be the brainchild of Mr. H.K. Nanjaiah, an entrepreneur in the field of the Pharmaceutical industry.

Since its inception in 2013, after a decade, the management of Kaade Hospital has come under the wings of care of our Chairman.
Since then, our humble attempt has been to support Kaade on 4 pillars of strength- i.e Integrity, Ethics, Morality, and Compassionate care.
