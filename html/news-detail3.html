From Missed Periods to First Kicks – Your Full Journey Through Pregnancy Kaade Hospital, Bengaluru – Where Every Mom’s Journey Begins with Care

Introduction

It starts with a missed period... A simple missed period can bring excitement and anxiety. Is it pregnancy? What comes next? This is where Kaade Hospital steps in — to guide you gently from that first doubt to delivery day and beyond.

First Trimester: Confirm & Care

Feeling nauseous, tired, or moody? These could be early signs of pregnancy. Visit our OBG team for:

• Confirmation scans

• Early tests

• Nutritional advice

We offer private consultations to make every mom feel safe and heard.

Second Trimester: Planning & Peace

As energy returns, it’s time for:

• Routine anomaly scans

• Diet and lifestyle tracking

• Discussing your birth plan

Kaade’s specialists support you with fetal monitoring, personalized care, and emotional readiness.

Third Trimester: Prepping for Baby

With the finish line in sight, moms may face:

• Back pain

• Swelling

• Anxiety about delivery

Beyond Birth: Care for You & Baby

Our job doesn’t end at delivery. We support:

• Postpartum recovery

• Breastfeeding guidance

• Newborn care

In case your baby needs extra care, our NICU is ready with advanced life-saving technology.

Why Kaade?

• 24x7 OBG & Pediatric care

• High-risk pregnancy support

• Advanced labor & neonatal facilities

• Comfortable rooms for every budget

Final Note

Motherhood is a journey — and you don’t have to walk it alone.

Kaade Hospital is here, every step of the way.
