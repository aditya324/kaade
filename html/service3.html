Comprehensive Medical Specialities

If you need a doctor to address your healthcare needs, Kaade Hospital provides a range of specialized services delivered with compassion and expertise.

Endocrinology

Comprehensive hormonal evaluation and treatment for various endocrine disorders.

Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology is the branch of medicine that studies the structure, function, and diseases of the digestive system.

Gastrointestinal Surgery

Expert surgical care for digestive tract disorders using advanced, minimally invasive techniques.

Urology

State-of-the-art urological care addressing disorders of the urinary tract and male reproductive system.

Paediatric Surgery

Specialized surgical care for infants, children, and adolescents with a focus on safety and comfort.

Reconstructive Surgery

Innovative surgical techniques to repair and restore form and function following injury or disease.

Oncology

Expert oncology care with advanced treatments and compassionate support. Trust us for personalized cancer care and recovery.

Eye Specialist

Specialized eye care services for a range of vision and eye health issues.

Cancer Surgery

Advanced cancer surgery with expert surgeons ensuring precise treatment and better recovery outcomes.

Nephrology

Comprehensive nephrology care for kidney health, offering expert diagnosis and advanced treatment.

Neurology

Expert neurology care for brain, spine, and nerve disorders with advanced diagnosis and treatment

Neuro and Spine Surgery

Advanced neuro and spine surgery for precise treatment, ensuring better mobility and recovery.

Plastic , Burn Surgery

Expert plastic and burn surgery for reconstructive, cosmetic, and trauma care with advanced techniques.
